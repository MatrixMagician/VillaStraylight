<objective>
Add the BENCH-04 read-only `villa bench --compare` / `villa bench --list` surface to the cmd-tier bench verb. `--list` enumerates saved reports; `--compare` loads saved reports, runs the pure `benchstore.Compare` comparability guard, and prints per-metric pp/tg deltas on separate lines — OR, on a non-comparable pair, prints "not comparable" + the differing fields and NO delta. Read-only: never runs a benchmark, never touches the backend, and rejects combination with the live-measurement flags at the cobra boundary. Exit mapping mirrors preflight: comparable delta → 0, not-comparable → 2, no/insufficient reports → 1 (with remediation). The `--compare --json` machine contract is byte-frozen by its own golden.

Purpose: BENCH-04 — compare saved reports gated by a comparability guard that refuses misleading deltas across non-comparable runs.
Output: extended `cmd/villa/bench.go` + `cmd/villa/bench_test.go` + new `cmd/villa/testdata/bench-compare.json.golden`.
</objective>
